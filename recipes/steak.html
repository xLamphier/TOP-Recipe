Steak

Ingredients

  • One 16oz new york strip steak
  • Butter
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Prepare steak by salting both sides and setting on a plate in the fridge for at least 30 minutes and up to 24 hours
  2. Take steak out of the fridge and let warm to room temperature at least 10 minutes before cooking blotting off any excess moisture with a paper towel
  3. Turn your burner on to medium-high and set cast iron or alternative pan on the burner, coating with a high smoke point oil like vegetable
  4. Once the pan is hot enough to be lightly smoking sear the steak on one side for about 3 minutes
  5. Once seared desired amount turn the heat down to about medium while flipping and searing the other side of the steak
  6. Add a tablespoon of butter to the pan once it has cooled slightly making sure the steak is able to take on some of the butter flavor
  7. Once steak hits 120 degrees F, take it off the pan and let rest for at least 5 minutes before eating

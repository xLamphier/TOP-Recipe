Chop Cheese

Ingredients

  • Hamburger Meat(1 lbs)
  • French Rolls
  • Cheese, American and Pepperjack
  • Butter
  • Salt, Pepper, other seasonings

Directions

  1. Turn stove onto medium heat and drizzle pan with oil coating entirety
  2. Pack meat into a circular patty season one side with salt and pepper
  3. Place patty seasoned side down and sear on on side, season the other side once its placed down in the pan
  4. Once fully seared on one side flip over and sear the other side for about 30 seconds
  5. Break patty up into small chunks and make sure the non-seared pieces are browned, make sure to keep the beef in a relatively roll-like shape
  6. Place a slice of pepperjack and a slice of american cheese down on the now reformed chopped up meat
  7. Toast your french roll in a toaster or on the pan
  8. Build your sandwhich however you like once the cheese is melted

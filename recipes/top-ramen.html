Top Ramen

Ingredients

  • One package of instant ramen of your choice
  • One egg
  • A pot of water

Directions

  1. He the pot of water to a boil
  2. Following the instructions on the package cook the ramen noodles until almost al dente
  3. Once noodles are mostly cooked reduce the water to the level you want and crack one egg into the pot
  4. Let the noodles finish cooking while trying not to break the egg up too much
  5. Drain water to desired level and stir in ramen seasoning packet as instrucked by the package
